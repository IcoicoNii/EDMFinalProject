📊 Dataset Overview

This dataset contains sales transaction data for across various locations and sales mediums, covering three years from 2018 to 2020. It includes detailed information about each sale, including the product name, revenue generated, sales medium/channel, and quantity sold.

Summary

🧾 Rows: 18,115

📊 Columns: 9

Source

The dataset was obtained from a publicly available repository hosted on GitHub by Hannatu Gwabin. This dataset is provided as part of a sales analysis project and contains structured transaction data for Nestlé product sales across Australia.

Potential Use

Analyze the sales performance of Nestlé products by year or region. Compare the effectiveness of online vs. direct sales. Evaluate customer preference trends over time and generate insights for regional marketing strategies.

Geographic

Sales data is broken down by Australian states and territories, enabling regional performance insights. This supports geographic segmentation and comparison between high and low-performing areas.
